TERMS OF SERVICE

PAYMENT

  1. All payments will be made to PayPal.
  2. All prices are in USD.
  3. Depending on the complexity of your request, additional fees may be added.
  4. For orders exceeding $1000, I am obligated to include Value Added Tax (VAT) in my invoices. [Full Name and Address required]

PRICING

  1. The price list is subjected to change in the future.
  2. Depending on the complexity of your request, additional fees may be added.

COMMERCIAL USE

  1. You must declare whether the commission is of personal use or commercial use.
  2. If the commission will be used for the purpose of promoting a brand, marketing, merchandising, and/or will stand to receive monetary gains, additional fees will be added.

MY RIGHTS

  1. If there is a deadline, I will do my best to follow it. If I can't do it until that deadline I will let you know.
  2. I will ask you for confirmation on anything I am unclear of before/during working on the commission so that I can draw your character the closest to your imagination.
  3. I have the right to decline a commission that I don't feel comfortable to do/not be able to do.
  4. I have the right to ask you for extra fees if there is anything that arises differently from our initial agreements.
  5. I have the right to cancel the commission at any time if I feel we cannot agree on the details of the commissions. If that happens when I have already worked on the commission, I will only refund you partially, based on how much I have worked on it.
  6. As long as it's not agreed otherwise between you and me, I have the right to use the commission work however I want, with proper credits given to you when needed.
  7. I can stream the process of your commission.

YOUR RIGHTS

  1. You need to make sure to give me all the information I will need to draw your character.
  2. You need to tell me when you send the payment so that I can check and confirm with you and add you to my working list.
  3. Please be patient with me, animation takes a long time.
  4. You may not make profits from the artwork in any way, unless you have declared the commission under commercial use.
  5. You can repost the finished picture to your gallery/sites, please credit me.
  6. You can note me anytime for an update on the commission's status/WIPs, or if you have any questions.

REVISIONS

  1. You will receive a preview for confirmation at each step (sketch, lineart, base color, etc.)
  2. Before I continue, you can still request me to fix things.
  3. I won't be able to return to a previously agreed step to fix, so please be as decisive as possible.

CANCEL/REFUNDS

  1. You can cancel a commission BEFORE I start working on it.
  2. If I am unable to complete the commission, you will be refunded fully.
  3. No refunds for completed artwork.

NFT + AI

  1. None of my artwork may be used to create any form of NFT, cryptocurrency, and/or recreated in any sort of artificial intelligence learning programs.
